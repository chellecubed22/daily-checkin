## What this is

A **short voluntary daily research diary** for self-reported wellbeing.

Participants answer the same brief items each day so patterns can be studied over time (sleep, energy, mood, stress, and optional context). Entries are for **personal / exploratory research** by the study lead—not clinical care.

**Time:** about 1–2 minutes  
**Device:** phone or computer  
**Cost:** free  

---

## What we ask (form v2.0)

| Area | Items |
|------|--------|
| Participant | Stable **participant ID** (same nickname every day) |
| Sleep | Duration (hours) + **quality** (1–10) |
| Daytime self-report | Energy/alertness, mood valence, stress (1–10) |
| Optional | Social connection, unusual-day flag, notes, next action |
| Consent | Required acknowledgment (voluntary, not medical) |

---

## What this is *not*

- Not a medical device, diagnosis, or therapy  
- Not a crisis service  
- Not a claim to measure planetary, geophysical, or “energy field” outcomes  
- Not required; stop anytime  

---

## How to participate

1. Open: https://chellecubed22.github.io/daily-checkin/  
2. Use the **same participant ID** every day  
3. Submit once per day (or as often as you choose)  
4. Skip days if needed—no penalty  

---

## Privacy (plain language)

- Submissions are sent to the **study lead’s project inbox** for logging and analysis.  
- Do **not** enter passwords, government IDs, or details you do not want stored.  
- Data may be summarized in aggregate (e.g. averages over time). Individual open-text notes are treated as private research material.

---

## For collaborators / scientific readers

This instrument is a **lightweight daily diary** suitable for:

- Habit and adherence pilots  
- Exploratory correlations with public environmental indices (weather, space weather) *as context only*  
- Building a multi-week self-report time series before any larger study design  

It is **not** presented as a validated clinical scale battery. Constructs are transparent 1–10 ratings plus sleep duration/quality.
